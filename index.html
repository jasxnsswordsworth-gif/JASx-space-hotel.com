About Us

Journey Across Space eXploration

Redefining luxury tourism for the new space age.

Executive Summary

Our project, JASX, is inspired by the ambition of SpaceX to revolutionize space technology. Our mission is to provide an international standard space tourism experience. Located in Middle Earth Orbit (MEO), the JASX hotel accommodates 3,000 guests with a total operational population of 4,000. We ensure every traveler, regardless of background, can experience the wonders of space under our professional guidelines.
