About Us

Journey Across Space eXploration

Redefining luxury tourism for the new space age — combining safety, sustainability, and unforgettable views.

Executive Summary

Our project name is JASX — Journey Across Space eXploration. JASX explores luxurious, sustainable, and accessible orbital tourism. We combine emerging reusable rocket technologies (inspired by current industry leaders) with hospitality design to deliver safe and memorable experiences. Our focus is safety-first engineering, modular habitats for different budgets, and an immersive guest experience.

Our Hotel Tier

Galaxy Grill

Designed for affordability. Our budget-friendly tier ensures space travel is accessible to a broader audience while maintaining safety and comfort.

Orbit Haven

The middle-tier experience. Superior comfort with a balanced combination of luxury, privacy, and advanced technology.

Nebula Royale

Ultra-luxury. Exclusive dining, premium suites, and white-glove service for the ultimate vacation in orbit.
